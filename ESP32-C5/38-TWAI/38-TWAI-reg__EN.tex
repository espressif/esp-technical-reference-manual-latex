In non-ISO FD or standard CAN, stuff bits are counted until the end of the frame. Note that this field is NOT Gray-coded as specified in the ISO FD standard. In non-ISO FD or standard CAN, de-stuff bits are counted until the end of the frame. Note that this field is NOT Gray-coded as specified in the ISO FD standard. The de-stuff count is updated during both transmission and reception. (RO)
