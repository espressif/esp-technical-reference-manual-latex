The addresses in parenthesis besides register names are the register addresses relative to the AES base address provided in Table \ref{table:Peripheral_Address_Mapping} \textit{Peripheral Address Mapping} in Chapter \ref{mod:sysmem} \textit{\nameref{mod:sysmem}}. The absolute register addresses are listed in Section \ref{sec:aes-reg-summ} \textit{\nameref{sec:aes-reg-summ}}.
